You and Me Always Forever

When we first started, we both thought — damn, everything’s going so smoothly, and it’ll always stay this way. But life? Life has a funny way of reminding us that it’s never just one note.

I know you probably thought I forgot about our 6-month anniversary. Maybe you even cried. And if you did, I can imagine exactly how that felt. But baby, no — I haven’t forgotten a single thing.

These past six months with you? They’ve been nothing short of extraordinary. From the little fights to the long-overdue talks, from moments of silence to bursts of laughter — this relationship has seen it all. And I’m just grateful I got to go through all of it with you.

Because if it hadn’t been you, I honestly don’t think I could’ve loved anyone else. No one else would’ve been you.

Shreya, you are — without question — the most beautiful woman on this earth to me. And I guarantee you that feeling will never change.

I want to see you grow. I want to watch you evolve. Get fat, get old, cry over a sad movie, or scream at me because I forgot to take the food off the stove. I don’t care. I’ll still love you the same. You’ll always be my pretty baby.

I’ve written so many things for you over these past six months. And yet, it all feels like so little compared to what you’ve given me.

You gave me a new life.

I was in a dark place — depressed, overwhelmed, done with everything. And then you came into my life and snapped me out of it. Not literally, of course. But in a way only you could, it was like you shouted, “Uth ja, bkl,” straight into my soul — with that fire, that Shreya energy no one else has.

And that’s when I fell for you. Truly. Fully. Irrevocably.

From that moment until now, and for every moment that’ll come after, I’ve loved you — and I will keep loving you.

Thank you, Shreya. For everything. For every single thing you’ve ever done for me, even the things you didn’t realize meant so much.

Someday, in a few years, I’ll ask you something really important. And I hope you’ll say yes.

You’re my beautiful baby. You always have been. You always will be.
